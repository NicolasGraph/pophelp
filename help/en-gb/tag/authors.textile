h1. Authors

bc. <txp:authors>

The *authors* tag is a _single_ or _container_ tag that Textpattern will use to gather a list of authors designated within the Textpattern "Users administration panel":http://docs.textpattern.io/administration/users-panel.

If used as a _container_ tag, it must be specified as an opening and closing pair of tags, like this:

bc. <txp:authors>
    ...contained statements...
</txp:authors>

"More information on the *authors* tag":http://docs.textpattern.io/tags/authors.
