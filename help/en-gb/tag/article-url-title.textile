h1. Article url title

bc. <txp:article_url_title />

The *article_url_title* tag is a _single_ tag which returns the dumbed-down 'URL title' of the article being displayed. This URL title may also be part of the page's address depending on the 'Permanent link mode' chosen in the "Preferences administration panel":http://docs.textpattern.io/administration/preferences-panel.

"More information on the *article_url_title* tag":http://docs.textpattern.io/tags/article-url-title.
