h1. Articles: Override form

If you wish for an article to use a form different from that assigned by default - for example if you want to show only an article excerpt with <a href="http://docs.textpattern.io/tags/excerpt" target="_blank">@<txp:excerpt />@</a> where you normally show the entire article with <a href="http://docs.textpattern.io/tags/body" target="_blank">@<txp:body />@</a> - this can be set here.
