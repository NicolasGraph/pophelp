h1. Textile: Headings

To make an entire paragraph into a heading, place <code>hn.</code> at its beginning, where <em>n</em> is a number from 1 - 6.

h3. Examples

bc. h1. Heading 1

bc. h2. Heading 2

bc. h3. Heading 3

Will become:

bc. <h1>Heading 1</h1>

bc. <h2>Heading 2</h2>

bc. <h3>Heading 3</h3>

Which, when rendered by a browser, will appear like this:

h1. Heading 1

h2. Heading 2

h3. Heading 3

p(alert-block information). <strong>Note:</strong> Quick tags must open and close within a single paragraph to produce valid markup.
