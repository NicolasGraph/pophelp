Textile: Headings

To make an entire paragraph into a heading, place hn. at its beginning, where n is a number from 1 - 6.

Examples

h1. Heading 1
h2. Heading 2
h3. Heading 3

Will become:

<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<h3>Heading 3</h3>

Which, when rendered by a browser, will appear like this:

Heading 1

Heading 2

Heading 3

Note: Quick tags must open and close within a single paragraph to produce valid markup.
