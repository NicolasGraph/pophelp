h1. Setup: User password

Since this account has the highest level of access in Textpattern, choose a very strong password or, preferably, a passphrase.

h3. Tips

* Use a mixture of uppercase and lowercase characters
* Use numbers
* Use symbols
* Make it at least eight characters in length
* <strong>Avoid</strong> any personal information (dates, names, places, family members, pets, etc.)
* <strong>Avoid</strong> any sole or repeated dictionary words, regardless of spelling, language or case
