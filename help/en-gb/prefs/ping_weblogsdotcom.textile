h1. Preferences: Ping pingomatic.com (Ping-O-Matic)?

This will send a <a rel="external" target="_blank" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ping_(blogging)">ping</a> to <a rel="external" target="_blank" href="http://pingomatic.com/">Ping-O-Matic</a> whenever you publish a new article. Ping-O-Matic is a aggregation service to notify listed websites that your website has been updated.
