Textile: Citations

Use ??double question marks?? around citations, such as the title of a work being cited.

The HTML tag is applied:

Nabokov’s ??Pnin?? → Nabokov’s <cite>Pnin</cite>

Note: quick tags must open and close within a single paragraph to produce valid markup.
